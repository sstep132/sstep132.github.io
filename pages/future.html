The Future is Bright for HTTP

In the future, old legacy systems will continue to be upgraded, and the use of HTTP/3 will continue to grow because it enhances performance and security. Since internet traffic is increasing and data is becoming websites are becoming more advanced daily, companies will have to make changes to their systems if they want to take advantage of the improvements that HTTP/3 provides. The switch will not be immediate because companies must deal with the challenges of updating old hardware and software to make it compatible with the new technology. The promise of faster data transfer, lower latency, better encryption, and support for today’s web applications will cause a shift from older versions of HTTP to HTTP/3 in order to meet the demand for faster, more reliable, and more secure communication. (The Pi Guy Blog, 2024).
